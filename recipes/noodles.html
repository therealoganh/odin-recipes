Easy Cheesy 2 Minute Noodles!

Super Quick and easy cheesy noodle recipe

This remarkably easy 2 minute noodle recipe will have your kids in love with dinner time!

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 20g butter
  • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 4 middle bacon rashers, trimmed, roughly chopped
  • 1 tbsp plain flour
  • 4 x 72g packets Maggi 2 Minute Noodles Chicken Flavour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 1 cup grated Devondale Colby Cheese Block (500g)
  • 1/4 cup fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, chopped
  • 1 tsp lemon zest

Steps

  1. Heat oil and butter in a large, deep frying pan over medium high heat. Add onion and bacon. Cook, stirring occasionaly, for 5 minutes or until onion softens. Transfer 2 tablespoons onion mixture to a small bowl. Cover to keep warm.
  2. Add flour and 2 of the chicken flavor sachets from noodles to pan (discard remaining flavor sachets). Cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Reduce heat to medium. Gradually stir in milk and 2 cups water. Bring to a simmer. Add noodles. Cook, stirring occasionally to break up noodles, for 2 minutes or until noodles are tender.
  3. Add peas and cheese. Cook for a further 1 to 2 minutes or until peas are bright green and tender. Add parsley and lemon zest to reserved onion mixture. Season with salt and pepper. Toss to combine.
  4. Remove noodles from heat. Sprinkle with onion mixture. Serve.
