America's Favorite: PBJ Sandwich

Recipe for the easiest snack ever!

Who doesn't love a good old fashioned peanut butter and jelly sandwich?

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter (crunchy or smooth, your choice)
  • Jelly (flavor of your choice, usually grape)
  • Sandwich bread (white or wheat)

Steps

  1. Lay out two slices of your sandwich bread.
  2. Using a butter knife, spread the peanut butter onto one slice of bread.
  3. Spread the jelly onto the other slice of bread.
  4. Combine the two slices.
  5. Cut off the crusts. (optional)
  6. Cut the sandwich in half.
  7. Enjoy your sandwich!
